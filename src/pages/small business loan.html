Small Loan is a product which is offered to a female entrepreneur only. The core objective of this product is to
empower the women in the country so that they can contribute to the economy effectively.

The key features of this loan are:

  1. The loan is for expansion of small businesses only.
  2. The loan is for expansion of small businesses only.
  3. Develops a self-help mentality and self-employment capacities of the entrepreneurs.
  4. Makes the underprivileged communities self-reliant through involving in income-generating activities
